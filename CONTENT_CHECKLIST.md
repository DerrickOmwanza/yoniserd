# WEBSITE CONTENT CHECKLIST

To ensure the timely completion of the YoNISeRD website, please provide the following items.

## 1. BRANDING ASSETS
- [ ] **Logo:** High-resolution file (PNG, SVG, or AI format).
- [ ] **Brand Colors:** Specific color codes (Hex/RGB) if any updates are needed.
- [ ] **Fonts:** Preferred font files or names (if different from current).

## 2. GENERAL INFORMATION
- [ ] **Contact Details:** Official phone numbers, email addresses, and physical location.
- [ ] **Social Media Links:** URLs for Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube.
- [ ] **Legal Documents:** Privacy Policy and Terms of Service text (if available).

## 3. PAGE-SPECIFIC CONTENT

### Home Page
- [ ] **Hero Image:** High-quality image for the main banner.
- [ ] **Welcome Message:** Short introductory text (2-3 sentences).
- [ ] **Key Statistics:** Updated numbers for "Years of Impact", "Communities Served", etc.

### About Us
- [ ] **Mission & Vision Statements:** Final approved text.
- [ ] **History:** A brief story of how YoNISeRD started.
- [ ] **Leadership Team:** Names, titles, bios, and photos of key team members.
- [ ] **Awards:** List of recognitions with dates and descriptions.

### Programs
- [ ] **Program List:** Names and descriptions of all current programs.
- [ ] **Beneficiary Stories:** Short quotes or testimonials for each program.

### Our Work / Gallery
- [ ] **Project Photos:** High-quality images from recent events (labeled).
- [ ] **Case Studies:** Detailed stories of specific projects (Problem, Solution, Impact).

### News & Stories
- [ ] **Initial Articles:** 3-5 blog posts or news updates to launch with.
- [ ] **Press Releases:** Any official statements to include.

### Impact
- [ ] **Reports:** Annual reports or impact summaries (PDFs to download).
- [ ] **Data:** Specific metrics to visualize (e.g., "500+ Youth Trained").

## 4. TECHNICAL ACCESS
- [ ] **Domain Name:** Access to the domain registrar (if already purchased).
- [ ] **Hosting:** Access to hosting account (if already set up).
- [ ] **Email:** Configuration details for the official email address.

## DELIVERY TIPS

1. **Pacing:** Speak clearly and moderately—not too fast, not too slow (approximately 120-150 words per minute)
2. **Tone:** Professional yet warm and approachable
3. **Emphasis:** Emphasize key numbers and achievements to build credibility
4. **Pauses:** Pause after each major point to let information sink in
5. **Enthusiasm:** Show genuine excitement about the organization's work
6. **Personalization:** Add your own personality while maintaining professionalism

## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Practice once before recording** - Read through the narration once
2. **Warm up your voice** - Drink water, do some vocal exercises
3. **Use a second monitor or phone** - Keep script visible while recording
4. **Record in segments** - Break into 2-3 videos if needed, then combine
5. **Multiple takes** - Don't pressure yourself to get it perfect first try
6. **Let music play softly** - Optional background music adds professionalism (keep at 20-30% volume)
7. **Test audio first** - Do a 30-second test recording to check quality
8. **Emphasize numbers** - These are impactful, speak them clearly
9. **Natural pauses** - Sound more professional with brief pauses between sentences
10. **Smile while speaking** - Affects your voice tone positively

================================================================================
                          TIPS FOR SUCCESS
================================================================================

NARRATION:
  - Speak at 120-150 words per minute (natural pace)
  - Use natural pauses between sentences
  - Emphasize important numbers: "Seventy-FIVE percent"
  - Show genuine enthusiasm for the organization
  - Smile while speaking (affects voice quality)

RECORDING:
  - Close all unnecessary applications
  - Put phone on silent
  - Don't rush‚Äîtake your time
  - If you make mistakes, pause and repeat
  - Mistakes can be edited out later

AUDIO QUALITY:
  - Mic positioned 6-12 inches from mouth
  - Quiet room (no AC, fans, wind, people)
  - Test record 30 seconds first
  - Monitor volume level (not too loud, not too quiet)

PACING:
  - Home page: 60 seconds
  - About page: 225 seconds
  - Programs: 50 seconds
  - Our Work: 60 seconds
  - Impact: 70 seconds
  - News: 55 seconds
  - Contact: 50 seconds
  - Closing: 20 seconds
  - Total target: 8-10 minutes

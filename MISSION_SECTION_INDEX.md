## üéØ Common Questions Answered

### Q: Is there only one mission section?
**A**: Yes. Single `id="mission"` section. No duplicates. See **MISSION_SECTION_VERIFICATION.md** line "Duplicate Removal"

### Q: How is it centered?
**A**: Flexbox on both axes. `display: flex`, `alignItems: center`, `justifyContent: center`. See **MISSION_SECTION_VISUAL_GUIDE.md** "Layout Centering Visualization"

### Q: What are the font sizes?
**A**: 5xl ‚Üí 8xl heading, xl ‚Üí 4xl mission text, lg ‚Üí 3xl vision text. See **MISSION_SECTION_VISUAL_GUIDE.md** "Responsive Typography Scale"

### Q: What animation is used?
**A**: Fade-in-up (0.8s ease-out). Heading at 0ms, text at 0.2s. See **MISSION_SECTION_VISUAL_GUIDE.md** "Animation Timeline"

### Q: Is it mobile-responsive?
**A**: Yes. Fully responsive with 4 breakpoints. Tested on 375px, 768px, 1024px, 1920px. See **MISSION_SECTION_VERIFICATION.md** "Responsiveness"

### Q: Is it accessible?
**A**: Yes. WCAG 2.1 AAA compliant. 7.5:1 contrast, semantic HTML, ARIA labels. See **MISSION_SECTION_VERIFICATION.md** "Accessibility Features"

### Q: Can I customize it?
**A**: Yes. See **MISSION_SECTION_README.md** "Customization Guide"

### Q: Is there an issue?
**A**: Check **MISSION_SECTION_README.md** "Common Issues & Solutions"

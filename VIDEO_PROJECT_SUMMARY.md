## RECORDING TIPS

**Pacing:**
- Speak at 120-150 words per minute
- Pause naturally between sentences
- Not too fast (people can't read on screen)
- Not too slow (sounds robotic)

**Emphasis:**
- Emphasize numbers: "Seventy-FIVE percent"
- Emphasize achievements: "Fifty COMMUNITIES"
- Show genuine enthusiasm for the work

**If You Make Mistakes:**
- Just pause 2 seconds
- Repeat the sentence
- You can edit it out later (or just delete and re-record that section)

**Tone:**
- Professional but warm
- Genuine interest in the organization
- Not robotic or monotone
- Smile while speaking (improves voice quality)

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: How long will it take to create the video?**
A: 15-50 minutes depending on which method you choose (Loom is fastest at 15 min)

**Q: Do I need to be an expert video editor?**
A: No! Loom and OBS do most of the work for you. No editing needed.

**Q: What if I make mistakes while narrating?**
A: Pause 2 seconds and repeat the sentence. You can edit it out or re-record.

**Q: What file format should I save as?**
A: MP4 with H.264 codec (most compatible format)

**Q: Can I add background music?**
A: Yes, but keep it at 20-30% volume so narration is primary

**Q: How do I share a large video file?**
A: Use cloud storage (Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox) and share the link instead of email attachment

**Q: Can I add text overlays or titles?**
A: Loom, DaVinci Resolve, and OBS all support this (optional)

**Q: What if my internet is slow?**
A: Use OBS Studio or Windows tools instead of Loom (they work offline)
